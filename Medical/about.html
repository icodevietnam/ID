About us

We are a specialist developer and producer of high quality medical devices focussed on the needs of anaesthesia and critical care professionals around the world.

Our goal is to enhance rather than replace the skills of medical professionals, reduce patient trauma, help eliminate cross contamination, and improve communication and efficiency in the clinical training environment. Our team recognises its first responsibility is to the patients and customers who experience our products.

We continue to develop our medical technology Intellectual Property platform and have core interests in airway management and patient monitoring technologies. We are widely acknowledged as a leader in medical product design and are committed to expanding our product range and markets in the coming years, and to building a significant new brand in healthcare.
